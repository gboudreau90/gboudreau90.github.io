CSC106 Final - Fruit Collector 1: Greyson Boudreau

About My Game

Fruit Collector 1 is a game that attempts to create a simple interactive game for children.
The main objective of the game is to collect 100 apples as fast as you can (but no pressure through a timer).
This games intention is to create a happy experience for young children with computers while not being overwhelming.
The game also serves to keep parents sound because of its harmless and simple nature.
